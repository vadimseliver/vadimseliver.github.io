1. Data Collection and Telemetry

NULL_SUBS is designed as a 100% offline application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal, financial, or behavioral data. The application contains zero telemetry, zero trackers, and no third-party analytical frameworks.

2. Local Storage

All subscription data, costs, and billing cycles are stored strictly locally on your physical device. Your data never leaves your device unless you manually use the export feature to create a local JSON backup.

3. System Permissions

The application requests permissions for Notifications and "Schedule Exact Alarms". These are used exclusively on your device to trigger local background timers that remind you of upcoming billing cycles. No data is sent to our servers regarding these timers.
